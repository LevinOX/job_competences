Stellenbeschreibung



You make the difference. Whether in business Audit & Assurance, Risk Advisory, Tax & Legal, Financial Advisory or Consulting: at Deloitte, we help our global clients continuously evolve. Discover a diverse working environment that is constantly in motion and always revolving around one central hub: you and your expertise. What impact will you make?


## Exciting tasks await you

- Supporting projects during all stages of development, including requirements analysis, design, implementation, testing as well as go-live
- Realisation of flexible AWS architectures in agile teams in compliance with clean code and good software crafting, suitable refactoring measures and continuous improvement of the build and test infrastructure
- Development of best practices for testability, maintainability and scalability to support an open team culture
- Active support in the knowledge transfer of best practices as well as mentoring of developer teams
- Advice and active technical support of internal teams as well as clients with the assessment of technologies to be used
- Participation in the Community, e.g. by actively taking part in conferences or meetups



## Your skill set

- Several years of experience in programming using Python, Java or Scala (full stack) and more than three years of experience as a software developer in the AWS cloud environment
- Extensive experience with implementing cloud infrastructure solutions (especially IaaS with AWS CloudFormation or Terraform) as well as certifications in the field of AWS (e.g. AWS Developer, AWS Architect)
- Extensive knowledge in the field of containerisation (e.g. Docker, Kubernetes) and experience with implementing microservices and security concepts in cloud-based applications
- Practical knowledge of and experience with continuous integration and deployment (e.g. git, Jenkins, SonarQube, etc.), with using Linux/Unix-Shell as well as cloud deployment models at an enterprise level (especially public, private, hybrid)
- Successfully completed bachelor’s or master’s degree in informatics and/or equivalent training
- Good (B2) German and very good English skills
- Moderate willingness to travel (occasional journeys to client meetings)



## What you can expect from us

In addition to a pleasant, collegial atmosphere, we offer you a promising and international working environment. We have flat hierarchies and an open door atmosphere. Another plus point: Thanks to a large number of education programs, we take you one big step forward in your professional and personal development.
